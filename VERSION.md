  * Apply
  * ApplyParagraphs
  * CollapseSpace
  * Indent
  * Insert
  * InsertDefinitionsTable
  * InsertTwoColumns
  * Justify
  * Wrap

  * Chars
  * CharsFrom
  * CharsTo
  * Lines
  * LinesFrom
  * LinesTo
  * Commit
  * CommitAll
* Options available:
  * IndentStr
  * LineSeparator
  * NoTrailingLineSeparators
  * ParagraphSeparator
  * LineSeparator
